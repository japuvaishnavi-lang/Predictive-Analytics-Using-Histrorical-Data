# Predictive Analytics Using Historical Data
## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing historical sales data and building an interactive Power BI dashboard to identify trends, evaluate business performance, and forecast future sales. The dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions using predictive analytics techniques and interactive visualizations.
## Objectives
* Analyze historical sales performance
* Identify trends and patterns in business data
* Forecast future sales using historical records
* Evaluate regional and category-wise performance
* Support data-driven decision-making
## Tools Used
* Power BI
* Microsoft Excel
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
## Dashboard Features
* Total Sales KPI
* Total Profit KPI
* Total Orders KPI
* Average Customer Satisfaction
* Average Discount
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Product Category Analysis
* Profit by Region
* Sales by Region Map
* Marketing Spend vs Sales Correlation
* Sales Growth Percentage
* Interactive Filters (Date, Region, Category)

## Key Insights
* Sales have shown a consistent upward trend over the last 5 years.
* Electronics is the top-performing product category contributing the highest share of total sales.
* The West region generates the highest profit compared to other regions.
* Marketing spend has a strong positive correlation with sales performance.
* Forecasting indicates continued sales growth over the next 6 months.
* Customer satisfaction remains consistently high.
## Project Outcome
This dashboard demonstrates how predictive analytics can be applied to historical business data to forecast future sales, identify business opportunities, and improve strategic decision-making.
